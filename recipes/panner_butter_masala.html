Panner Butter Masala

Description

paneer butter masala is rich and creamy made of butter, paneer, onions, tomatoes, cashew and spice powders, and herbs. adding a fresh and generous amount of butter to the gravy gives a good taste.

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Cashews
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Chilli and garam masala

Steps

  1. Roast the onions and tomatoes in oil until fried
  2. Add garam and chilli masalas to taste
  3. Toss in a few cashews and roast everything again together
  4. Finally blend into a fine paste, followed by adding paneer and water
